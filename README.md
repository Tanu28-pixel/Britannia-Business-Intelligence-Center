# Britannia Business Intelligence Center Dashboard

## Overview

The Britannia Business Intelligence Center Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, inventory management, customer behavior, and brand contribution within the FMCG retail sector.

This dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators, identify revenue drivers, optimize inventory levels, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objective

To analyze retail business performance by evaluating revenue, profit margins, inventory health, customer loyalty, brand contribution, and city-wise sales trends.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* SQL
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence
* DAX

---

## Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Margin
* Units Sold
* Average Margin
* Stock On Hand
* Loyalty Score

---

## Dashboard Features

### Revenue & Margin Trend Analysis

Tracks monthly revenue and margin performance to identify growth patterns and profitability trends.

### Top 5 Brand Contribution

Visualizes the revenue contribution of leading FMCG brands.

### Revenue by City

Highlights top-performing cities based on revenue generation.

### Inventory Command Center

Monitors stock availability, lead time, and reorder levels to support efficient inventory management.

### Top 10 Brands by Revenue

Provides a comparative analysis of brand-wise business performance.

### Executive Insights

Summarizes key business findings and actionable insights for stakeholders.

---

## Key Insights

* ITC leads all brands in revenue contribution.
* Kolkata is the highest-performing city by revenue.
* Total revenue exceeded 39M with strong profitability.
* Inventory levels remain healthy at 27M units.
* Reorder levels support uninterrupted supply chain operations.
* Top-performing brands and cities contribute a major share of total revenue.
